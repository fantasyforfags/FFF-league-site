FFF LEAGUE CONSTITUTION

Article I — League Overview

1.1
The purpose of this League is to provide a competitive, fair, and entertaining fantasy football experience for all Members. League rules are established to maintain integrity, consistency, and enjoyment throughout the Season.

1.2
All Members agree to follow the rules set forth in this Constitution. Any disputes, loopholes, or unforeseen circumstances will be resolved by the Commissioner, whose decisions are final unless otherwise specified.

Article II — League Governance

2.1 Commissioner Role
The Commissioner administers the League, enforces rules, manages disputes, oversees the draft, and ensures fair play.

2.2 Commissioner Powers
The Commissioner has the authority to:
– Enforce all rules within this Constitution
– Issue warnings, penalties, and corrective actions
– Reverse collusion, illegal trades, or clear errors
– Make final decisions when rules are unclear

2.3 Commissioner Limitations
The Commissioner may NOT:
– Change scoring settings mid-season without unanimous consent
– Approve his own trade (requires 100% approval from all other Members)

Article III — League Membership

3.1 Eligibility
Any individual invited by the League may participate. Returning Members have priority.

3.2 Expectations of Members
All Members must:
– Actively manage their lineup
– Respond to League communication
– Participate in draft, waivers, and playoffs
– Demonstrate fairness and sportsmanship

3.3 Removal of a Member
A Member may be removed if:
– They repeatedly fail to set lineups
– They engage in collusion
– They violate rules or disrupt League integrity

Removal requires a two-thirds (⅔) vote of all other Members.

Article IV — League Finances

4.1 Buy-In
The buy-in amount is decided before each Season by unanimous vote.

4.2 Prize Payouts
Default payout structure:
– 1st Place: Champion payout
– 2nd Place: Runner-up payout
– 3rd Place: Third place payout

4.3 Payment Deadlines
All Members must pay buy-ins by Week 1 kickoff or face:
– Warning
– Temporary lockout from trades/waivers
– Potential removal

Article V — Draft Rules

5.1 Draft Format
The League uses a standard snake draft unless unanimously changed.

5.2 Draft Order
Determined by:
– Random draw
– Lottery
– Previous season standings

5.3 Draft Timer
Missed picks result in:
– Auto-draft
– Or Commissioner selecting BPA

5.4 Draft Conduct
Cheating, collusion, or outside interference is prohibited.

Article VI — Trades

6.1 Trade Approval
All trades require Commissioner review.

6.2 Trade Veto System
Trades may be vetoed if:
– Majority votes to veto
– Collusion is suspected
– Competitive balance is threatened

6.3 Trade Deadline
Follows the platform’s standard date unless changed pre-season.

Article VII — Waivers & Free Agency

7.1 Waiver Order
Decided pre-season as:
– Rolling priority
– FAAB
– Reverse standings

7.2 Waiver Conduct
No manipulating waivers via collusion or arranged drops.

7.3 Illegal Activity
Any attempt to exploit waiver timing may result in reversal or penalty.

Article VIII — Rookie Regulations

8.1 (A)
A Rookie is any Member playing in the League for the first time.

8.1 (B)
Rookies may be required to pay a reduced buy-in or rookie fee.

8.2 (A)
If a Rookie wins the Championship, they receive only half (½) of the standard champion’s prize.

8.2 (B)
The other half rolls into the next Season’s prize pool.

Article IX — Collusion & Cheating

9.1 Definition
Collusion includes:
– Coordinated trades
– Purposely losing
– Lineup manipulation
– Waiver manipulation

9.2 Punishments
Penalties may include:
– Reversal of actions
– Loss of draft picks
– Fines
– Removal from League

Article X — Tanking

10.1 Prohibited Behavior
Members may NOT intentionally lose games by:
– Benching starters
– Starting injured players
– Ignoring lineups

10.2 Penalties
Commissioner may:
– Fix lineups
– Issue penalties
– Adjust draft order
– Remove playoff eligibility

Article XI — Rule Changes

11.1 Off-Season Rule Changes
Require ⅔ vote.

11.2 Mid-Season Rule Changes
Require unanimous vote.

11.3 Constitutional Amendments
Require unanimous vote & Commissioner approval.

Article XII — Playoffs

12.1 Format
Standard playoff format unless changed pre-season.

12.2 Tiebreakers
1. Head-to-head 2. Total points 3. Bench points 4. Coin flip

12.3 Integrity
Playoff teams must set optimal lineups and maintain activity.

Article XIII — Sportsmanship

13.1 Conduct
Members must show respect, integrity, and fairness.

13.2 Trash Talk
Trash talk is allowed but cannot become harassment.

Article XIV — Final Authority

14.1 Commissioner Final Say
The Commissioner has final authority over all disputes.

14.2 Integrity Clause
All decisions must prioritize fairness and League integrity.
